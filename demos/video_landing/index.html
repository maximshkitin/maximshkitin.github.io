School of Rock

Workshop

Always wanted to be part of a band? We can teach you how, in just a day or less. Want to unleash your inner diva but too shy to sing in front of a crowd? We got auto-tuned microphones for you.

Participants will get to experience learning a variety of instruments in different teams and once perfection is reached, teams will gather back and play together as a band. Depending on your preference, teams can play together as a Big Band or have a mini competition between the teams.

In this programme, participants will learn the importance of teamwork and coordination while having fun. Best of all, no musical experience is required at all. Our experienced instructors are all highly sought-after musicians in the industry.
