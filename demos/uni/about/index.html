Background story this event

The Asia Business Leaders Awards organization, (ABLA), created by global business broadcaster CNBC in 2001, recognizes the unique abilities and successes of top business leaders in the region. The awardees are selected every year by an objective panel of top international business people, management strategists and academics who look closely at each nominee’s past demonstration of strength, innovation and foresight. Since it’s inception 15 years ago, ABLA has presented 78 Asian Business leaders with these coveted awards.

For the first time, rather than looking at past accomplishments, ABLA will now be looking into the future.

The new “I Am Future” Unicorn Award will go to the one young tech company, which the judges believe has the potential to become a Unicorn within the next 6 years.
