About Us

Our dedication towards high quality combat illuminated sabers

SaberMachis a Singapore brand, focusing on building high quality combat illuminated sabers. All SaberMach products are manufactured in-house and made for one purpose - combat dueling. The ergonomic design of each saber provides the saberlist with maximum comfort while dueling. At SaberMach we are not just all about sabers. We are also manufacturers of premium quality custom designed and themed acrylic display cases. Our acrylic cases are highly detailed and custom designed and can be tailored made to each customer's unique preferences. Above all we provide all types of acrylic fabrication and engraving needs. Sabermach continues to strive to bring new original concepts and designs for a complete and holistic approach to collecting and displaying sabers and collectibles.
