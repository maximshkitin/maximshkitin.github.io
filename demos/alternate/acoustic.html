Acoustic Guitar

Our passion for teaching guitar is to impart the skills of real-world application, basic music theory, instrument maintenance, knowledge about the instrument itself, and to truly be a student of the guitar.

Many times, the content in instructional method books can be quite different to what is being applied by musicians. The books are technically not wrong, but over time, you will realize what you learn in these books are not what is being used by musicians.

We believe that if you learn an instrument, you should also learn some music theory as well. This will equip you to communicate in a more precise and articulate way with other musicians. Therefore, the notes that we have prepared for our students contain music notations, which they will learn how to read. You will also have the necessary skills to be an all rounded musician and even if you choose to pursue a full-time study in music, you can be assured that you’ll be ready for it.

What you can expect to learn

  1. Your favourite songs

    Have a song you’ve been wanting to play? We’ll help you get there

  2. Strumming & Plucking techniques

    Learn all different strumming and plucking patterns that you’ll ever need and best of all, we will teach you how to create your own.

  3. Guitar Chords

    Learn different chord progressions, chord substitutions, how are they formed and how to create your own chords

  4. Music Theory

    Set your foundation right, understand and interpret written music and translate that into your playing.

  5. Scales

    Learning scales will strengthen your fretting hand and build coordination between your fingers. It also allows you to move between chords and scale patterns easily.

  6. Pop Song Transcription

    Develop skills to transcribe any songs in the future. In addition, our instructors are able to provide transcription for you based on your level of playing, at no additional cost.

  7. Different Genres such as:

    • Pop
    • Rock
    • R&B
    • Shuffle
    • Bosa Nova
