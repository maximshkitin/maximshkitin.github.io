Bass Lesson

Lesson 1

Develop your own sound, and become a well-rounded bassist through our well-thought out curriculum and original course materials developed by our experienced instructor.

We adhere to the belief that there is no fix way in learning. Every student has his unique learning styles and experiences. Our instructors have meticulously designed the course in a structured manner, yet remains tailored and adapted to every student’s needs and goal.

Join us in this musical journey experience as we build your strong foundation, cover the fundamentals and help you progress to advance level in your playing skills.
